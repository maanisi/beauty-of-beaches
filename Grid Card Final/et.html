West Beaches

Piha Beach

Piha is popular with experienced surfers, but it’s also a wonderful spot for picnics, relaxing walks and swims – under the watchful eye of the famous Piha Surf Club. See Lion Rock, a small island that stands in the middle of the beach.

Download

Karekare Beach

Karekare is one of Auckland's most magnificent beaches, its isolation adding to its stunning natural beauty. Part of the Waitākere Ranges Regional Park, Karekare was made famous by the Oscar-winning movie The Piano.

Download

Te Henga (Bethells Beach)

Te Henga (Bethells) is smaller than the other beaches, with a cosier feel. The sand begins well back from the surf and there is a shallow lagoon before the river reaches the sea.
